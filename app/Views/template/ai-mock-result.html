1. Why did you choose this specific university?

Your Answer | timer 0:45s

"I chose the University of Washington because it has a really big campus and I like the location in Seattle. Also, the computer science program is very famous, and I think it will be good for my future career."

AI Insight & Better Answer

Critique: Your answer is generic. Officers want to hear about specific academic fit, not just location or fame.

"I selected the University of Washington specifically for its Human-Computer Interaction research lab led by Professor X. This aligns perfectly with my goal to specialize in accessible UX design, which isn't available at universities in my home country."

2. How will you fund your education?

warning Weak

Your Answer | timer 0:22s

"My father will pay for everything. He has a business. It is a good business, so money is not a problem for us."

lightbulb

Improvement Needed

Why it failed: "Business" is too vague. You need to specify income sources and liquid assets.

"My education is fully sponsored by my father, who owns a textile manufacturing firm with an annual turnover of $150,000. We have saved $60,000 in liquid assets specifically for my first year, as shown in these bank statements."
